SMS Messaging Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Effective Date: February 13, 2026  |  Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Introduction

MMT Financial & Insurance ("MMT," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of our team members and contacts. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our SMS text messaging program.

2. SMS Messaging Program Description

Our SMS messaging program is used for internal business notifications related to our Warm Market Prospect Intake Form system. Messages are sent to MMT team members and leadership to notify them when a prospect form has been submitted. Team members who submit a prospect form may also receive a confirmation text acknowledging their submission.

3. Consent & Opt-In

By providing your mobile phone number on the MMT Warm Market Prospect Intake Form and submitting the form, you consent to receive SMS text messages from MMT Financial & Insurance related to your form submission. Consent is obtained through our web-based intake form hosted at our official website.

Providing your phone number and consent is voluntary. You are not required to opt in to SMS messaging as a condition of doing business with MMT Financial & Insurance or participating in our team activities.

Important: The opt-in consent checkbox on our intake form is optional and is never pre-checked. You must actively check the box and submit the form to opt in.

4. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. Team members will receive an SMS notification each time a prospect form is submitted (typically a few messages per week). Submitting team members receive one confirmation message per form submission.

5. Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Please consult your mobile carrier's rate plan for details. MMT Financial & Insurance is not responsible for any fees charged by your wireless provider.

6. Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and will no longer receive SMS messages from this program.

8. Information We Collect

Through our SMS messaging program, we may collect:

  • Mobile phone numbers provided voluntarily by team members
  • Opt-in and opt-out status
  • Message delivery and response data

9. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

  • Sending SMS notifications about prospect form submissions
  • Sending submission confirmation messages to team members
  • Managing opt-in and opt-out preferences
  • Improving our internal business communications

10. No Sharing or Selling of Mobile Information

We will never share, sell, rent, or disclose your mobile phone number, opt-in status, or any mobile-related information to third parties, affiliates, lead generators, or any other entity for marketing or promotional purposes. Your mobile information is used solely for the purposes described in this policy.

11. Prospect Information Confidentiality

All prospect information submitted through our Warm Market Prospect Intake Form is treated as strictly confidential. MMT Financial & Insurance will never contact any prospect without the expressed authorization of the team member who submitted their information.

12. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the personal information we collect. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Data Retention

We retain mobile phone numbers and opt-in records for as long as you are an active participant in our SMS program. Upon opting out, we will retain your opt-out request for compliance purposes and delete your information from active messaging lists.

14. Children's Privacy

Our SMS program is intended for adults (18 years of age or older). We do not knowingly collect mobile numbers from individuals under the age of 18.

15. Supported Carriers

Our SMS program is supported on all major U.S. wireless carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

16. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued participation in the SMS program after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
